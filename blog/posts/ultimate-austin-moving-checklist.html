The Ultimate Austin Moving Checklist: Your 8-Week Timeline

September 22, 2024 Moving Tips 8 min read

Moving to Austin doesn't have to be overwhelming. With proper planning and our comprehensive 8-week timeline, you'll be enjoying BBQ and live music in no time. This checklist has helped thousands of families make a smooth transition to the Live Music Capital of the World.

8 Weeks Before Moving

  • Research Austin neighborhoods - Consider factors like commute times, school districts, and lifestyle preferences
  • Get moving quotes - Request quotes from multiple Austin moving companies
  • Create a moving budget - Factor in moving costs, deposits, and immediate expenses
  • Start decluttering - Donate or sell items you won't need in your new home

6 Weeks Before Moving

  • Book your moving company - Popular Austin movers book up quickly, especially during peak season
  • Order moving supplies - Boxes, packing materials, labels, and markers
  • Notify your landlord - Give proper notice if you're renting
  • Research Austin utilities - Austin Energy, water services, internet providers

4 Weeks Before Moving

  • Schedule utility transfers - Set up services at your new Austin address
  • Update your address - Bank accounts, credit cards, subscriptions
  • Research Austin schools - If you have children, understand enrollment processes
  • Plan time off work - Moving day and settling-in time

2 Weeks Before Moving

  • Confirm moving details - Double-check dates, times, and services with your mover
  • Pack non-essential items - Seasonal clothes, books, decorations
  • Research Austin DMV requirements - Texas vehicle registration and driver's license
  • Find Austin healthcare providers - Doctors, dentists, veterinarians

1 Week Before Moving

  • Pack most belongings - Leave only essentials for the final week
  • Confirm utility connections - Ensure services will be active on move-in day
  • Prepare cash for tips - Budget for tipping your Austin moving crew
  • Clean out perishables - Use up food that won't make the move

Moving Day

  • Be present for the move - Stay available to answer questions and direct movers
  • Do a final walkthrough - Check all rooms, closets, and storage areas
  • Keep important documents with you - Don't pack birth certificates, passports, or financial records
  • Take photos - Document the condition of your belongings and new home

First Week in Austin

  • Update your Texas voter registration - Register within 30 days of moving
  • Get a Texas driver's license - Required within 90 days for new residents
  • Register your vehicle - Texas requires registration within 30 days
  • Find your new favorite spots - Explore local coffee shops, restaurants, and entertainment
