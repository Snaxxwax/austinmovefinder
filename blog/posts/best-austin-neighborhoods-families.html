Best Austin Neighborhoods for Families: A Complete Guide

September 20, 2024 Neighborhoods 12 min read

Choosing the right neighborhood is one of the most important decisions when moving to Austin with your family. From top-rated schools to family-friendly amenities, we've researched the best communities that offer the perfect blend of Austin's unique culture and family-centered living.

What Makes a Great Family Neighborhood in Austin?

When evaluating Austin neighborhoods for families, we consider several key factors:

  • School ratings and districts - Academic performance and extracurricular offerings
  • Safety and crime rates - Community security and neighborhood watch programs
  • Parks and recreation - Green spaces, playgrounds, and family activities
  • Commute times - Access to major employment centers
  • Community feel - Neighbors, local events, and family-friendly businesses

Top Austin Neighborhoods for Families

1. Cedar Park

Why families love it: Cedar Park consistently ranks among the best places to live in Texas, offering excellent schools, low crime rates, and a strong sense of community.

  • Schools: Leander ISD with multiple 9/10 rated schools
  • Housing: Mix of newer developments and established neighborhoods
  • Activities: Cedar Park Center, numerous parks, youth sports leagues
  • Commute: 25-30 minutes to downtown Austin

2. Round Rock

Why families love it: Known for its excellent schools and family-oriented community, Round Rock offers suburban comfort with easy access to Austin.

  • Schools: Round Rock ISD with highly rated elementary and high schools
  • Housing: Variety of home styles and price points
  • Activities: Dell Diamond baseball stadium, extensive trail system
  • Commute: 20-25 minutes to downtown Austin

3. Westlake

Why families love it: Luxury living with some of the best schools in Texas, Westlake is perfect for families seeking top-tier education and amenities.

  • Schools: Eanes ISD - consistently top-rated in Texas
  • Housing: Upscale homes with higher price points
  • Activities: Lake Austin access, high-end shopping, country clubs
  • Commute: 15-20 minutes to downtown Austin

4. South Austin (Sunset Valley/Oak Hill)

Why families love it: Maintains Austin's quirky charm while offering family-friendly amenities and good school options.

  • Schools: Austin ISD with several highly rated schools
  • Housing: Mix of older homes and new developments
  • Activities: Zilker Park, food trucks, community festivals
  • Commute: 15-25 minutes to downtown Austin

5. Mueller

Why families love it: A master-planned community built on sustainability and walkability, perfect for modern families.

  • Schools: Austin ISD with new, well-funded schools nearby
  • Housing: Contemporary homes with green building standards
  • Activities: Mueller Lake Park, farmers market, community events
  • Commute: 10-15 minutes to downtown Austin

Important Considerations

School Districts

Austin area school districts vary significantly in ratings and offerings. Research specific schools within districts, as quality can vary by campus.

Housing Costs

Family-friendly neighborhoods typically command higher prices. Budget for property taxes, which can be significant in Texas.

Traffic and Commuting

Austin traffic can be challenging. Consider commute times during rush hour and factor in growth projections for your chosen area.
